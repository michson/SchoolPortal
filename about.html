School History

BREIF HISTORY OF THE SCHOOL

Graceland Polytechnic, Offa was established in 2013 on the initiative of the President CEO Princess (Dr). Ayodele Olufemi Oyeleke. The Polytechnic is set to provide full time and part time courses of instruction and training in Technology, Applied Science, Commerce, Management, Industrial, Agricultural Production and Distribution, Research in the development and adaptation of technique as the Governing Council may from time to time determine. It is to arrange conferences, seminars and study groups relative to the fields of learning and to perform such other functions as in the opinion of the Governing Council may serve to promote the objectives of the Polytechnic.

The Management of the Polytechnic is governed by the Governing Council, Principal Officers, Academic Board and Directors of Schools/ servicing Divisions.

Mission

M

Graceland Polytechnic Offa, has the following Mission Statement

  1. To provide high quality technological and entrepreneurial education to promote self-reliance for development.
  2. Our core values are quality and hard work for both lecturers and students to produce timely results.
  3. We have passion for excellence and expect our students to aim towards performance based reward and recognition system.
  4. Zero tolerance for all forms of indiscipline while encouraging accountability and innovativeness from all concerned.

Vision

V

Our vision is to develop an Institution that inspires passion for learning development of appropriate technical skills for high productivity and economic growth.

Governing Council

G

The Polytechnic Governing Council is responsible for the general management of the polytechnic. The President is the Chairman of the Governing Council. The Council is responsible for the control of finances and properties of the Polytechnic and is empowered to enter into contracts on behalf of the Polytechnic. The Council is also responsible for the appointment of all academic and administrative staff, including the Rector. The Council determines policy, but is not involved with the day to day running of the Polytechnic. The Governing Council may consist of the following:

  1. Congregational Representative,
  2. Academic Board Representative,
  3. Alumni Representative,
  4. Rector & the two (2) Deputy Rectors,
  5. Registrar serves as the Secretary.

Academic Board

A

The Academic Board is a statutory body. It has the Rector as its chairman and the Registrar as the secretary. Heads of Academic Departments are members of the Board.

Principal Officers/Management

MR. LAWAL
Acting Rector

RESPONSIBILITIES

The Rector is the Chief Academic and the Chief Administrative Officer of the Polytechnic. He/she is also the Chief Accounting and Chief Security Officer of the Polytechnic. He/she has statutory powers to exercise general authority over the staff and has responsibility for the discipline in the Polytechnic. He/she is the Chairman of the Academic Board. He/she is responsible to the President/Chairman Governing Council.

The discipline of the students of Graceland Polytechnic is conferred on the Rector and the Polytechnic Governing Council.

MR. IBITOYE T. Abiodun
Registrar

RESPONSIBILITIES

The Registrar is responsible to the Rector for the day to day administration of Graceland Polytechnic. He is the Secretary to the Governing Council and all its committees. He is also the Secretary to the Academic Board. He is the custodian of all records and the Polytechnic Seal. The Registrar also services all departments by deploying supporting staff as may be required by the departments.

MR.
Bursar

RESPONSIBILITIES

The Bursar is the Chief Financial Officer of Graceland Polytechnic who is responsible and accountable to the Rector. The Bursar is responsible for advising the Rector on the proper financial management of the Polytechnic and current Federal Government instructions and Treasury circulars when the Polytechnic is affected.

MR. AYO SALAMI
Librarian

RESPONSIBILITIES

The Librarian is responsible to the Rector for all issues in connection with the Library.
